About me

Hello! I am a second-year PhD Researcher in Economics at the European University Institute (EUI), where my research spans cultural economics, social identities, and economic history. I earned a joint Master’s Degree in Economics and Political Science from Bocconi University and Sciences Po in 2021. Prior to starting my PhD, I worked as a Pre-Doctoral Fellow in Economics at the University of Zurich (UZH).
