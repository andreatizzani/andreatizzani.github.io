Valeria Solesin Award 2022

Andrea Tizzani
Nov 26, 2022 1 min read
Il Sole 24 Ore

I am thrilled to announce that in 2022, I became one of the proud recipients of the prestigious Valeria Solesin Award, with my Masterâ€™s thesis entitled “Women Water and War: A Geospatial Analysis For Gendering Domestic Peace in Africa”, a work that delves into the crucial interplay between gender dynamics and civil conflict in continental Africa.

The Valeria Solesin Award, named in memory of the Italian researcher tragically lost in the Bataclan massacre of November 13, 2015, honors outstanding Master’s dissertations that explore themes around female talent and its pivotal role in shaping economies, ethical frameworks, and meritocratic principles. Valeria, who was conducting her research at the Sorbonne of Paris, devoted her studies to understanding the dual role of women juggling family and professional life.

This award symbolizes more than just an academic achievement; it’s an embodiment of Valeria’s enduring legacy and a testament to the potential of female talent as a cornerstone for socio-economic development.
